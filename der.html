Enough! We Want the Hylians Out of Power!

The lush, rich, ample Golden Lands of Termina have nourished our brothers and sisters, our fathers and their fathers, for generations on end. No matter what race, tribe, or group you come from Termina Field has always been a home for anyone who needs it. But sadly, not any longer.

Since the Hylian Occupation began, countless tribes have been driven further and further from the lands that nourished them. The results of the increasing Hylian Settlements have pushed most of them into decline, where not outright driving them to extinction. We need only remember the Kokiri for proof of that.

But we are not like them. We do not put ourselves above them like they do over everyone else. We want to co-exist with the Hylians, as the Giants always meant for us to do. But before that can happen they MUST stop their war of cultural supremacy against the rest of the world.

This is what we believe:

  • The Four Giants, in Their melancholy and weariness, laid down the foundations of the world and handed the reins over to the First Four races equally.
  • We believe that the Giants intend for us to share dominion of the world with all the races that inhabit it.
  • We will fight, die, and do anything in our power to achieve this. The Giants are good.

The Unwritten History of "Hyrule"

According to known Hylian history, The Goddess Hylia was left in charge of a portion of this world some time after the Giants laid the Foundations. Her people, the Hylians, lived in Termina for generations, though a name for the place before The Exile is not recorded anywhere in Hylian history.

For an age, Hylia ruled over the then-unnamed land that was then known only as "the Surface," presiding over the various races in the world at that time.

This sounds unlikely, for one, because "The Surface" is a name that only makes sense when looking down on the world from the sky. Clearly, this is the name for the world that the Hylians coined, only later, when they were in exile in the sky.

One possibility is that the name was lost over the ages. A likely and completely plausible scenario. Another possibility is that the name was recorded, but that Hylian historians don't want you to know what that name is. But I want you to know. It was Termina.

I know this because every other tribe race and clan throughout the world called it Termina. If the Hylians were truly masters of the world as they say, then it must have been them that disseminated this name to the other races to begin with. If not, it is a name that reminds that Hylians that they didn't always live here.

But whatever it was called, it is without question that in the beginning, the Hylians lived here in Termina with us all. But it didn't last. Soon a great war engulfed the entire land.

A fissure opened in the earth, connecting the underground to the Surface. From this fissure poured an army of demons led by their ruler, the Demon King known as Demise.

According to Hylian lore, The Great Demon King Demise was the cause of the Great War that sent the Hylians into exile. However, histories from the Gorons, Zora, and Kokiri (before their extinction) tell us that a great number of other factors were at play leading to nearly every tribe on the face of Termina to take arms up against each other for one reason or another. Certainly the appearance of Demise and his forces from Below exacerbated the situation greatly and led to a much bloodier conflict than we would have seen otherwise. But the truth is, the Hylian population was already beginning to chafe at its boundaries, leading to skirmishes with several of the surrounding tribes. Had they been able to stay in balance with the world Demise wouldn't have found so many ready and willing allies, and he'd never have been able to get a foothold in our world.

But gain a foothold he did, and Termina burned. The Hylians were under threat from all sides and with little other choice, Hylia sent her people into exile in the sky. The Hylians lived above it all for millenia. But after the defeat of Demise at the hands of the Skyward Hero, the Hylian people returned to Termina, re-christening it Hyrule, and claiming it all for themselves. Perhaps it was the time in exile led them to forget their origins, as one piece of a much more elaborate and beautiful puzzle, or perhaps they had just finally come to know themselves enough to admit what they wanted, total control and domination.

You may have noticed that what is not recorded in Hylian history is the fates of the races and tribes of this world while the Hylians were exiled to the sky. The reason is that it would be too damaging to Hylian morale, and luckily for them, you can't find it all in one place. Each of the peoples of Termina keep their own account of the ages, and you'd have to show them all enough respect to gain access to them. Something the Hylians seem to have a hard time with.

However, historians in the Free Termina movement have been starting to compile these books, accounts of The Return and the bloody war that led to the founding of Hyrule Castle. The tale of how the Gorons lost stewardship over the Golden Lands, and even the exact details in the extinction of the Kokiri. True Termina history has never before been available to the people, but thanks to the work we're doing to recover, translate, and compile numerous non-Hylian sources there will soon be a place to get a bigger picture, a truer picture, of this land and the multitudes of different peoples in it.
